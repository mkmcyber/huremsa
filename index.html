About HUREMSA

HUREMSA is the official association for Moi University students pursuing Human Resource Management. With over 100 active members, we are dedicated to empowering students, fostering professional growth, and promoting excellence in HRM.

Our Objectives

  • Promote academic and professional excellence in Human Resource Management.
  • Facilitate networking and mentorship opportunities for members.
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and events to enhance HRM knowledge and skills.
  • Encourage collaboration with industry partners and HR professionals.
  • Support community outreach and social responsibility initiatives.

Our Achievements

  • Successfully hosted 3 Annual Career Summits with over 20 guest speakers
  • Secured internship and attachment opportunities for over 50 members
  • Conducted company visits to leading HR firms in Kenya
  • Held 5 Team Building Retreats to enhance member bonding and leadership
  • Launched a mentorship program connecting students to alumni professionals
  • Organized successful mentorship programs with leading HR professionals.
